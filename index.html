🥤 Thirsty Games
Pick a drink and face the computer!

How to Play
• Choose one of the 19 drinks.
• The computer randomly chooses one drink.
• If both drinks are the same, it's a Tie.
• If the computer's drink appears in your drink's → list, You Win.
• Otherwise, You Lose.
